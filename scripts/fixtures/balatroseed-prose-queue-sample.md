# Balatro Seed: 6B678B4W (Plasma Deck)

*Small Blind*

* Skip for Mega Arcana Pack.
* Take Soul for Perkeo.
* Take High Priestess for Jupiter and Earth.
* Sell Earth while keeping Jupiter.

_Rest of Ante_

* Buy Seed Money Immediately
* Sell the negative Jupiter every round from here on out for eco.

**Ante 5**

_Before Small Blind_

* Buy Blank Tag.
* Take the Arcana Pack for Hanged Man on 4 and 5 of hearts.

_Before Big Blind_

* Sell Golden Joker and buy DNA.

_Before Boss Blind_

* Buy both Mega Arcana Packs.
* On the second use the Deja vu on the King.
* Use Chariot on the red seal king.

_Before Small Blind_

_Boss Blind_

* You should hit Cryptid here.

**Ante 8**

* Buy Telescope.
* Buy Celestial Pack for Pluto.
